# UAE-Government-Services-Assistant
Sabeel is an Agentic AI-powered Public Service Navigation Assistant designed to simplify complex government procedures and bureaucratic processes. The platform guides users through public services by providing personalized roadmaps, eligibility checks, document requirements, and recovery plans for missing information or documents.

The project aims to reduce confusion and improve accessibility for citizens, students, immigrants, and small business owners who often struggle to understand government processes
